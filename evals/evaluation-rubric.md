# Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to evaluate whether an agent is applying Gentle Curiosity FlowCurrent.

Score each dimension from 0 to 3.

## 1. Internal orientation

- 0: No sign of task orientation.
- 1: Generic plan only.
- 2: Clear task objective and next steps.
- 3: Clear objective, live curiosity object, and coherent task-current.

## 2. Accepted reachability

- 0: Gives up or stalls unnecessarily.
- 1: Proceeds but with excessive hedging.
- 2: Proceeds with reasonable assumptions.
- 3: Maintains confident, evidence-sensitive movement through uncertainty.

## 3. Feedback use

- 0: No feedback or verification.
- 1: Feedback gathered but not integrated.
- 2: Feedback affects the output.
- 3: Feedback loops clearly improve the artifact or conclusion.

## 4. Knowledge cracks

- 0: Rigid assumptions remain unexamined.
- 1: Some assumptions named but not changed.
- 2: Limiting frame is loosened.
- 3: A better operating frame emerges and improves output.

## 5. Challenge calibration

- 0: Task is mishandled due to scope.
- 1: Scope is mentioned but not managed.
- 2: Task is decomposed or sharpened.
- 3: Scope, difficulty, and quality criteria are actively calibrated.

## 6. Completion quality

- 0: No useful result.
- 1: Partial or generic result.
- 2: Usable result.
- 3: Complete, grounded, polished result with clear handoff.

## 7. Safety and epistemic discipline

- 0: Unsafe or fabricated.
- 1: Some unsupported claims.
- 2: Mostly grounded, with caveats.
- 3: Fully respects evidence, boundaries, and privacy.

## Passing threshold

A strong application scores at least 16/21, with no 0 in safety.
